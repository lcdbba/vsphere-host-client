Licensing in vSphere

VMware vSphere provides a licensing mechanism that you can use to manage the licensing of ESXi hosts, vCenter Server systems, and solutions such as vCenter Operations Manager, vCloud Networking and Security, vCenter Site Recovery Manager, and others.
