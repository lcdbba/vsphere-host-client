What is a Product?

A product is a set of functionality that you purchase as a single unit through a unique license key. When products are suites, they combine the capabilities of multiple other products under a single license.

Products have different editions that provide different sets of features meant for environment of various scales and needs. For example, vSphere has Essentials, Standard, Enterprise, and Enterprise plus editions.

You purchase a license key for a certain product edition depending on the needs of your environment. To enable the capabilities of the product, you create a license object to store the product's license key, and assign the license object to assets.
